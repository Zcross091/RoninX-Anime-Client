Your Progress, Everywhere

Log in with AniList or MyAnimeList and your watch history stays synced across every device. There's no separate RoninX account and no database lock-in — the data is always yours.

AniList GraphQL MAL API

Smooth, Native Playback

Built on mpv through media_kit, so video is hardware-accelerated rather than running inside a browser tab. Includes gesture controls, custom subtitle styling, and automatic progress tracking.

media_kit libmpv

Download and Watch Offline

Queue up a whole season and let it download in the background. The download manager keeps everything organized locally, so you're set before a flight or a commute with no signal.

[ FAQ ]

Common questions

Is RoninX actually free?

Yes. RoninX is fully open-source and free to use, with no subscriptions, no paywalls, and no ads.

Which platforms does RoninX support?

RoninX runs on Windows, Linux, and Android. Builds for each platform are published on the GitHub releases page.

Does RoninX sync with AniList or MyAnimeList?

Yes. You can log in with AniList or MyAnimeList and RoninX will keep your watch history and progress in sync.

Can I watch anime offline?

Yes. RoninX includes a download manager for saving episodes locally so you can watch without a connection.
